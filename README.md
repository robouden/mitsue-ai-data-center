# Mitsue Project (御杖プロジェクト)

**Sustainable Energy, Forest Restoration & Digital Infrastructure for Rural Japan**

📍 **Location:** Mitsue Village, Nara Prefecture, Japan  
📅 **Started:** April 2026  
🎯 **Timeline:** 25-year initiative  
🌐 **Status:** Phase 0 - Pre-Foundation

---

## Mission

To demonstrate that rural Japanese communities can build their own sustainable future by integrating ecological restoration, locally generated clean energy, and modern digital infrastructure — and to share what is learned so that other communities may follow.

## Vision

By 2050, Mitsue Village will be home to a self-sustaining model of rural revitalization in which:
- Restored native forests replace aged sugi (cedar) plantations
- Locally generated biomass and renewable energy power the community
- A small-scale, community-owned data center supports the digital economy
- The model is openly documented and freely available to any community

---

## Core Components

### 🌲 Forest Restoration
- Gradual replacement of aged sugi plantations with native broadleaf species
- Collaboration with private landowners and forestry authorities
- 25-year ecological restoration timeline

### ⚡ Sustainable Energy Generation
- Biomass and biogas production from sustainably harvested forest material
- Clean energy for local consumption and EV charging network
- Potential grid feed-in via FIT/FIP programs

### 💻 AI Data Center
- Repurposing the closed Mitsue Elementary School building
- Small-scale, energy-efficient edge computing focus
- Powered entirely by locally generated renewable energy

## Principles

1. **Local First** — All decisions begin with the wellbeing of Mitsue residents
2. **Open and Transparent** — All data, financial records, and methodologies public
3. **Patient and Long-term** — 25-year horizon, no premature scaling
4. **Replicable** — Documented for other communities to adapt
5. **Modest in Scale** — Small enough to remain accountable
6. **Non-partisan** — No political alignment
